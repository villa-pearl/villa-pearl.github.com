Family Apartment – ​​Comfort and Space in the Embrace of the Sea

The largest apartment of Villa Pearl is the ideal choice for those looking for spacious, bright and comfortable accommodation right on the beach. The apartment can accommodate up to five people, making it perfect for families or groups of friends.

The apartment has two separate bedrooms, a sunny living room, a fully equipped kitchen, a bathroom and a large terrace overlooking the sea, where guests can enjoy their morning coffee or watch the sunset.

Studio apartment A

This unique studio apartment was once a traditional Dalmatian “konoba” – a place where families cooked, ate, chatted and spent summer evenings together. Today it welcomes its guests as a cozy, ground floor apartment – ​​right at sea level.

The apartment consists of two separate parts:
– a spacious bedroom,
– and a separate room with a kitchen and bathroom.

In front of the house there is a huge terrace overlooking the sea, where you can enjoy Dalmatian feeling while having your morning coffee, afternoon relaxation or a romantic sunset.

Studio apartment B

Romantic sea view with sunset from bed
The Studio is located on the middle level of Villa Pearl, and its real specialty is that it opens up to the largest terrace in the house, from which you can enjoy a stunning view of the Adriatic Sea. The most beautiful moment is when you can watch the sunset from your bed in the evening – this studio is also a perfect choice for couples or those arriving with a child.

The apartment is airy, yet practical: the sleeping area is separated from the living room and kitchen by a sliding door, making the space comfortable and well-usable.
