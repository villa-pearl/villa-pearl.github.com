Studio apartment II

Romantic sea view with sunset from bed
The Studio is located on the middle level of Villa Pearl, and its real specialty is that it opens up to the largest terrace in the house, from which you can enjoy a stunning view of the Adriatic Sea. The most beautiful moment is when you can watch the sunset from your bed in the evening – this studio is also a perfect choice for couples or those arriving with a child.

The apartment is airy, yet practical: the sleeping area is separated from the living room and kitchen by a sliding door, making the space comfortable and well-usable.
